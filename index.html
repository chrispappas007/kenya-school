Kenyan School Attrition Rates

Data on the enrollment of children in primary school (grades 1-8) were compiled for the year 2014. The rates were parsed by gender (girls vs. boys) and totaled by county. Data was provided through the Kenyan Open Data portal and can be downloaded here.

Rates by Gender

Overall, enrollment rates between girls and boys have remained consistent. Boys out-number girls in most counties; however girls have higher enrollment rates in more populated counties such as Nairobi and Meru. In general, enrollment rates seem to decrease in the higher primary school grades in general, and especially in rural counties. The greatest disparity in enrollment between boys and girls is also found in the rural counties. There are likely a variety of factors that contribute to decreases in enrollment (entrance into the workforce, marriage, etc.) and future initiatives will need to mitigate these factors.
